STEP 01

Teaching Assistant Contracts Help: What Next?

Workload Creation

Created in FRIS by staff/supervisors. You'll receive an email when it's ready.

Review & Agree

Review details in FRIS. Contact supervisor with concerns before agreeing online.

Contract Phase

Contract is generated after workload agreement. Both must be signed to get paid.

Payment Setup

Update banking, address, and permits in MyHR/Unity once contract is uploaded.

Pop-up Blockers

Workload forms and contracts open in new windows. Ensure your browser's pop-up blocker is disabled for the FRIS site.

Updating Your Information

Status changes in FRIS to Uploaded and activates your profile in MyHR/Unity.

Important Login Note

You will be able to log in to MyHR/Unity only a day after the contract status becomes Uploaded.

Payment & Payroll

Pay periods typically end two weeks before the actual pay date. It is vital to have your information updated in MyHR/Unity to ensure timely payment.

Example Pay Schedule

If your contract starts June 13th and you work for one week:

First Pay Date: July 1st

If your contract ends August 26th:

Final Pay Date: September 9th

Support & Credentials

Setting Up Employee Netname

If you don't have an Employee Netname yet, use your Student Netname to create one in FRIS.

  1. Submit Identity Service form with Student ID.
  2. Verify your personal email.
  3. Wait for "Concordia Account Creation Success" email.
  4. Login to FRIS with your new credentials.
