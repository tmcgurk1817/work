STEP 04

Updating Your Information

After you sign, the contract status will be Dean Signed (Finalized). The office of the provost will then upload it from FRIS to the HR system.

This changes the status in FRIS to Uploaded and activates your profile in MyHR/Unity.

Important Login Note

You will be able to log in to MyHR/Unity only a day after the contract status becomes Uploaded AND from the start date of your contract.

Login via Carrefour Setup MFA (Required)

STEP 05

Payment & Payroll

Pay periods typically end two weeks before the actual pay date. It is vital to have your information updated in MyHR/Unity to ensure timely payment.

Example Pay Schedule

If your contract starts June 13th and you work for one week:

First Pay Date: July 1st

If your contract ends August 26th:

Final Pay Date: September 9th

Payroll Calendar

Download the full official payroll calendar to track all pay dates for the academic year.

View Calendar

Missing a payment?

Verify your direct deposit info in Unity and ensure your contract status is "Uploaded".

STEP 06

Support & Credentials

Setting Up Employee Netname

If you don't have an Employee Netname yet, use your Student Netname to create one in FRIS. The system will guide you through an identity verification form.

  1. Submit Identity Service form with Student ID.
  2. Verify your personal email via the code sent.
  3. Wait for "Concordia Account Creation Success" email (approx. 15 mins).
  4. Login to FRIS with your new credentials.
