Recurring Room Schedules

  • The calendars below show the teaching bookings for the current semester. It does not show ad-hoc bookings which may have been added.
  • The goal of the tools is to give users an idea of the likelihood of a given time slot for a given room.
  • If you enter a request for a room that is not available the department attempts to book you in an equivalent space.
  • You can also request preferred options in the notes section of the form (e.g., If not 1271 then 1267).
  • You will receive an invitation from Outlook Calendars once your booking is confirmed.
